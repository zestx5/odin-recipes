Pineapple-Apricot Mimosa

Description

Not your usual mimosa. I always get rave reviews when I make these.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) can apricot nectar
  • 1 (12 ounce) can pineapple juice
  • Â¾ cup water
  • 1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle Champagne

Steps

  1. Combine apricot nectar, pineapple juice, water, and frozen orange juice concentrate in a large pitcher and stir well. Chill until ready to serve. Stir in Champagne just before serving.
